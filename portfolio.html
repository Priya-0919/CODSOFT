Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking, also known as penetration testing or white-hat hacking, is the practice of legally and ethically exploiting vulnerabilities within computer systems, networks, or software applications.Unlike malicious hackers, ethical hackers leverage their skills to identify weaknesses and security flaws with the goal of assisting organizations in enhancing their cybersecurity measures.
